About Us

At Generous Rounds, we are dedicated to making a positive impact in our community and beyond. Our mission is to foster meaningful connections through innovative solutions that drive social good and environmental sustainability. We believe in empowering individuals and organizations to create lasting change.

What We Do

We provide resources, support, and technology-driven initiatives to enhance charitable giving and promote community engagement. Through our platform, we enable users to make effortless contributions to causes they care about, transforming small actions into significant impact.

How It Works

1. Sign Up: Create an account by providing basic information and linking your payment method.

2. Select Causes: Browse and choose from a variety of charitable organizations to support.

3. Select Causes: Round Up Purchases: Enable the round-up feature to automatically donate spare change from your purchases.

4. Track Donations: Monitor your contributions and view the impact of your donations on supported causes.

5. Receive Tax Receipts: Access your tax receipts directly through your account for easy record-keeping during tax season

6. Engage with Community: Share your experiences and see how others are making a difference.
